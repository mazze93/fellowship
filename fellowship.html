Why Fellowship exists
Today's systems optimize for notifications and engagement. Fellowship optimizes for human cognition.

By combining environmental signals, time-of-day awareness, and workflow context, Fellowship creates a living interface that supports deep work, creative wandering, learning, and recovery. Computers should understand when you're building, when you're exploring, when you're learning, and when you're resting — and shape themselves accordingly.

Five modes · seven environments

Each environment is drawn, not styled. Two turn with the day.

Every environment is a coordinated atmosphere — a target nervous-system state, a light strategy that supports it, and a specific failure it's calibrated against. Signal and Havens each carry a Day and a Night rendering; the previews below are the reference art itself.
